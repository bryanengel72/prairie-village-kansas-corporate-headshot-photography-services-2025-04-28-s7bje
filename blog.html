How to Prepare for Your Corporate Headshot Session

April 28, 2025

A professional corporate headshot is a crucial investment in your personal brand. Proper preparation ensures optimal results, starting with wardrobe selection - choose solid colors and timeless pieces that reflect your industry. Schedule grooming appointments strategically, with haircuts 1-2 weeks before the session. Practice basic posing techniques to feel confident, maintaining good posture and a natural expression. Mental preparation is key - understand your audience and desired message. Arrive early, bring touch-up supplies, and stay hydrated. After the session, take time to select the perfect image that authentically represents you while meeting professional standards.
