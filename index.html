Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a headshot session take?

Standard sessions typically last 30-45 minutes, including makeup application. Corporate group sessions are scheduled based on team size, with multiple shots per person to ensure the perfect image.

What should I wear for my headshot?

We recommend solid colors and professional attire that reflects your industry. Avoid busy patterns or bright colors that might distract from your face. We'll provide detailed guidance upon booking.

How quickly will I receive my photos?

Digital proofs are available within 48 hours, and final retouched images are delivered within 5 business days. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.

Do you offer retouching services?

Yes, professional retouching is included with every session. We focus on natural-looking results while maintaining your authentic appearance.

Can you accommodate large corporate teams?

Absolutely! We specialize in corporate team sessions and can photograph up to 30 people per day on-site. We'll work with your schedule to minimize disruption to your workday.
